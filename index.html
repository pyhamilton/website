Documentation

Automate your Biology Experiments

PyHamilton empowers you design and perform biology experiments using Python, a flexible and interactive programming language. This makes it easier than ever to redo, edit and share your experiments, allowing you to focus on what matters: designing experiments.

conda install pyhamilton

Features

Works on All Robots

Write your methods once, then run them on any supported robot.

Open Source

You have control software from commands down to the USB cable.

Use any Computer

Use any laptop to perform experiments, whether it runs on Windows, macOS or Linux.

Jupyter Notebooks

Easily prototype your experiments using Jupyter Notebooks.

Share your Research

With PyHamilton, people can easily build on your research.
